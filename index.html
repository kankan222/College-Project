About Kalong Kapili Vidyapith

Kalong kapili Vidyapith is located in the heart of Nagaon town. KKV has two sections : One is school section and the other is higher secondary section.The school section (Class I to X) is located at GM Road, behind Nowgong College and the higher secondary section (Arts, Science and Commerce stream) is situated at A.D.P. I am extremely delighted to welcome you to Kalong Kapili Vidyapith , the college with an excellent academic reputation since its inception .The college established in the year 2004, is symbolically named as Kalong Kapili Vidyapith , after the two important rivers Kalong and Kapili flowing through the heart of undivided Nagaon District .
