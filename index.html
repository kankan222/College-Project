About Kalong Kapili Vidyapith

Kalong kapili Vidyapith is located in the heart of Nagaon town. KKV has two sections : One is school section and the other is higher secondary section.The school section (Class I to X) is located at GM Road, behind Nowgong College and the higher secondary section (Arts, Science and Commerce stream) is situated at A.D.P.
